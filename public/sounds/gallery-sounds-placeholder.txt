# Gallery Sound Effects Placeholders

Place the following sound effect files in this directory:

## Required Sound Files

### 1. paper-rustle.mp3
- Description: Light paper rustling/movement sound
- Duration: 0.5-1 second
- Use: When hovering over gallery items

### 2. tape-rip.mp3
- Description: Masking tape being peeled off
- Duration: 0.5-1.5 seconds
- Use: When removing/deleting artwork

### 3. pin-push.mp3
- Description: Push pin being inserted into cork board
- Duration: 0.3-0.5 seconds
- Use: When favoriting artwork

### 4. paper-flip.mp3
- Description: Paper page turning/flipping
- Duration: 0.5-0.8 seconds
- Use: When clicking on artwork

### 5. cork-pop.mp3
- Description: Soft cork/bulletin board interaction
- Duration: 0.2-0.4 seconds
- Use: General interaction feedback

## Recommended Specifications
- Format: MP3
- Bitrate: 128kbps
- Volume: Normalized to -12dB
- Size: < 100KB per file

## Audio Creation Tips
1. Keep sounds subtle and non-intrusive
2. Add slight reverb for natural feel
3. Ensure clean start/end (no clicks)
4. Test at various volume levels
5. Consider accessibility (not startling)
